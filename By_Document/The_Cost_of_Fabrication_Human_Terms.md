## 2. Whole-Document Human Lyric

This document explains how Andy’s framework started.

The plain version is:

Andy ran into a severe AI hallucination loop while exploring a hard problem. That
experience made the core failure visible: an AI system can keep producing
confident output even when it has lost track of what is real, what is supported,
and what is allowed to continue.

Instead of treating that as a one-off failure, Andy treated it as a systems
problem. He began building a way to force work through structure:

- identify the object;
- name the constraint;
- find the weakest point;
- require a repair path;
- prevent unsupported closure;
- leave an audit trail.

The source describes how that method grew from personal experience into a
constraint-navigation architecture that could be applied to AI reasoning,
engineering audits, supply-chain bottlenecks, experimental campaigns, and
creative discovery.

The human meaning is not “this proves a universal theory.”

The human meaning is:

```text
This is the story of how a practical method for navigating ambiguous systems was
built out of direct exposure to failure, pressure, hallucination, and repair.
```
