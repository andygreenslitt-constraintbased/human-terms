## 2. Whole-Document Human Lyric

Admissible Movement is a way to ask:

```text
What can still be done from here without making the system less able to recover,
move, or govern itself later?
```

It is not trying to find the best answer.

It is not trying to score the situation.

It is not trying to replace legal, medical, engineering, safety, policy, or
domain review.

It gives a structured way to inspect movement before acting:

- action;
- delay;
- repair;
- continuation;
- non-action;
- redesign;
- exit.

The core human idea is:

```text
Sometimes moving forward is not the admissible move. Sometimes HOLD, REPAIR,
REDESIGN, LIMITED TRIAL, or EXIT review is the honest output.
```

This is useful for AI workflow evaluation, operations review, policy review,
process repair, and ambiguous decision environments because it prevents a
system from treating motion as progress when motion may be degrading future
governability.

## 10. One-Page Reader Version

### What this is

Structural Synthesis: Admissible Movement is a public framework packet for
reviewing what movement remains admissible under constraint.

It exists because action is not always progress. A system can look active while
it is degrading its future ability to move, recover, or govern itself.

It helps inspect ambiguous decisions where action, delay, repair, non-action,
continuation, redesign, or exit might each be possible, but some of those moves
